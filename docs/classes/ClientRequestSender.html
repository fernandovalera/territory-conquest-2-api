ClientRequestSender:exchangeArmies (army_from_id, general_ids, units, army_to_id)
Exchanges or splits armies.

Parameters:

  • army_from_id the army exchange units from
  • general_ids a list of General ids
  • units a dict where the keys are unit types and the values are the amount
  • army_to_id (optional) army to exchange units to

Returns:

    the new army id, if this was created
ClientRequestSender:mergeArmies (army_id_list, target_node_id)
Merges all armies specified and optionally moves armies to a target node.

Parameters:

  • army_id_list list of Army ids to merge
  • target_node_id (optional) node id to merge armies on

Send Armies.

Parameters:

  • army_id_list a list of Army ids

ClientRequestSender:trainArmy (node_id, army_name, units)
Train an army.

Parameters:

  • node_id id of the Territory to train the army
  • army_name the desired name for the army
  • units the desired unit composition of the army
ClientRequestSender:trainArmyRefill (structure_id, target_army_id, units)
Train refill Armies. Must be sent to an allied army

Parameters:

  • structure_id structure to use to produce units
  • target_army_id id of target army to refill
  • units units to produce

Buy a structure from the player's team's structure list.

ClientRequestSender:fetchStructureList (team_id)
Get structure list for team. Can return empty if team was invalid.

Parameters:

  • team_id id of the Team to fetch a structure list for
ClientRequestSender:declareWar (team_id)
Declare war with another team. No confirmation required.

Parameters:

  • team_id the id of the Team to affect
ClientRequestSender:endWar (team_id)
Attempt to broker a peace with another team. The other team will be notified and must agree for the war to end.

Parameters:

  • team_id the id of the Team to affect
ClientRequestSender:requestAlliance (team_id, minimum_duration)
Send an ally request to another team. The other team will be notified and must agree to form/join an alliance.

Parameters:

  • team_id the id of the Team to affect
  • minimum_duration the minimum duration in minutes to specify the alliance for
ClientRequestSender:breakAlliance (team_id)
Break an alliance with another team. No confirmation required.

Parameters:

  • team_id the id of the Team to affect
ClientRequestSender:openBorders (team_ids)
Open borders with a list of teams. Has no effect whilst at war or allied with another team.

Parameters:

  • team_ids the ids of the Teams to affect
ClientRequestSender:closeBorders (team_ids)
Close borders with a list of teams. Has no effect whilst at war or allied with another team.

Parameters:

  • team_ids the ids of the Teams to affect
